# 🏥 Healthcare Analytics Dashboard

An AI-assisted executive dashboard analyzing 55,500 patient records to surface clinical, financial, and operational insights for healthcare decision-makers.

👉 [Click here to view the live dashboard](https://sufaidahabeeb.github.io/healthcare-analytics-dashboard/)

## 📊 Project Overview

This dashboard analyzes a dataset of **55,500 patient records** to uncover insights across:
- Clinical outcomes
- Financial performance
- Hospital operations

The goal was to transform raw healthcare data into **structured, decision-ready insights** — the kind that support real executive and operational decisions in healthcare settings.

---

## 📌 Dashboard Sections

### 🔹 Overview
- Key KPIs (total patients, revenue, avg billing, length of stay)
- Monthly admission trends (24-month rolling)
- Admission type distribution (Elective / Urgent / Emergency)

### 🔹 Clinical Analysis
- Diagnostic outcomes (Normal vs Abnormal vs Inconclusive)
- Disease distribution across 6 major conditions
- Patient demographics (age, gender, blood type)

### 🔹 Financial Analysis
- Revenue by diagnosis ($238M peak for Diabetes)
- Insurance payer mix across 5 major providers
- Financial contribution by condition and billing averages

### 🔹 Operations
- Hospital network performance (top 8 facilities by revenue)
- Length of stay benchmarking vs industry standard
- Admission flow and emergency rate analysis

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- ⚠️ **33.6% abnormal test rate**, exceeding the standard 25% healthcare benchmark — flagged as a critical quality alert
- 💰 Total network revenue reached **$1.42B** across all conditions and payers
- 📈 **Diabetes** is the top revenue-generating condition at **$238.5M**
- 🏥 Average length of stay is **15.5 days** vs the industry benchmark of 5.4 days — a significant operational flag
- 🩺 Near-perfect payer diversity across 5 insurance providers (each ~20% share)

---

## 🧠 Approach

Built using an AI-assisted workflow that combines clinical domain knowledge with structured data analysis:

- Identified executive-relevant KPIs from raw, unstructured patient data
- Surfaced a critical diagnostic quality flag (33.6% abnormal rate) missed in raw form
- Designed four separate views tailored for clinical, financial, and operational stakeholders
- Translated healthcare data into business-language insights without losing clinical accuracy

This project demonstrates how a clinician's domain knowledge, combined with AI tools, produces sharper and more contextually accurate insights than data analysis alone.
